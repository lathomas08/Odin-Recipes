Salmon

This Pan Seared Salmon with Creany Lemon Pasta is a perfect date night dinner. Easy to make and ready in 15 minutes, this crispy salmon and pasta is delicious and beutfiul.

list of ingredients:

  • Salmon
  • Olive oil
  • White wine
  • Cream
  • Lemon juice
  • Parsley
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Shallots
  • Butter

Instructions for Salmon:

  1. Remove Salmon from the fridge for atleats 15 minutes.
  2. Rub the salmon with a little olive oil and season with salt.
  3. Use a non stick skillet of frying pan to ensure crisp on the salmon.
  4. Cook the salmon over medium-high heat.
  5. Cook the salmon top side down.
  6. Cook the salmon for 3 minutes on top side than two minutes on the other side.

Instructions for Lemon Cream Sauce

  1. Start by cooking a shallot gently in butter and then deglaze the pan with white wine.
  2. Once the wine has reduced, lemon juice is added.
  3. After, add the heavy cream
  4. Once this has come to a simmer, the sauce is finished with black pepper, lemon zest, and parsley.

Serving

  • Serve with your choice of fresh or boxed pasta.
  • Sides that could be served with the dish include Broccoli and Asparagus.
  • Place lemon sauce over the salmon and pasta.
